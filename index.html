Hello!

It’s already challenging enough trying to keep up with everything on your to-do list. We get it!

And it’s no fun when the Doc adds another thing or two to your list...

We built this app to help other people like us - who have trouble keeping up with all the little things in life, such as remembering to take daily medications.

Life shouldn't be so hard all the time... so make things simple with

Daily Dose

Popular Features

See some of the top rated features of Daily Dose!

Easy Access

Use Daily Dosed on all your devices, including your computer, tablet, and smartphone!

Secure Login

Use our safe and simple login feature to record and save all of your medications and notes.

Hassle Free

Ditch those pill containers! Keep your meds in the safe containers that they belong. We'll remind you when to pop those things out.

Healthier You

Stay on top of those hard-to-remember meds and never miss a dose again. Plus we'll have you taking them on schedule.

Better Days

With one less thing to worry about, you can focus more on your day and the things that matter to you most.

Top Priority

Our users will always be our top priority. We created this app out of love for helping people, that is why it will always be free.
